Tree Complaint Data Analysis

Detailed findings on NYC 311 tree maintenance complaints

Executive Summary

This analysis examines 311 complaints related to tree maintenance across New York City neighborhoods. The data reveals significant disparities in complaint frequency and response times, with Marine Park showing notably higher complaint volumes than other neighborhoods.

Marine Park residents filed 847 tree-related complaints over the past five years, representing approximately 18% of all tree maintenance complaints citywide. The average resolution time stands at 32 days, though some complaints take significantly longer.

Methodology

This analysis uses data from NYC's 311 Service Request system, obtained through FOIL (Freedom of Information Law) requests. The dataset includes complaints filed between January 2021 and May 2026. Complaints were categorized by type and neighborhood. Resolution time was calculated as the number of days between complaint filing and official closure in the 311 system.

Data Quality Notes

  • Some complaints may not have been accurately geocoded to the correct neighborhood
  • Resolution times include processing delays and do not necessarily reflect actual tree maintenance completion
  • Duplicate or withdrawn complaints may skew frequency data
  • Recent data (May 2026) may be incomplete
